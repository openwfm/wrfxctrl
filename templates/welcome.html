Fire forecasting control system

Welcome to the WRFx controller website, an experimental system built to initiate fire simulations using the wrfxpy system. The wrfxpy system in turn relies on WRF-SFIRE to perform the numerical simulation itself. The results of the simulation will appear on the integrated visualization system wrfxweb.
